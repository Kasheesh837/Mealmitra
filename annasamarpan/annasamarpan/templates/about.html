About Annasamarpan

Connecting hearts through food. Our mission is to ensure no meal goes wasted and no hand goes empty.

Our Story

Annasamarpan was born from a simple yet powerful belief: no meal should go wasted, and no hand should go empty. In a world where millions suffer from hunger while tons of food are wasted daily, we saw an opportunity to bridge this gap.

Founded by a group of passionate individuals who witnessed food waste firsthand, we created a platform that connects generous donors with dedicated volunteers and needy recipients. Our technology makes it easy for anyone to contribute to solving hunger in their community.

Today, we're proud to be part of a growing movement that's transforming how communities address food insecurity, one donation at a time.

Our Mission & Vision

We're driven by a clear purpose and an ambitious vision for the future

Our Mission

To eliminate food waste and hunger by creating a seamless connection between food donors, volunteers, and recipients. We believe that technology can amplify human compassion to solve one of society's most pressing challenges.

Our Vision

A world where no food goes to waste and no person goes hungry. We envision communities where sharing resources becomes second nature, creating a more equitable and sustainable society for all.
