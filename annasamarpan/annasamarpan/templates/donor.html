What Happens Next?

After you submit your donation, here's what we do to ensure it reaches those in need

1. Verification

Our admin team reviews your donation details and verifies the information provided.

2. Volunteer Assignment

We assign a nearby volunteer who will pick up your donation and deliver it to recipients.

3. Delivery

The volunteer picks up your donation and delivers it to verified recipients in need.

Donation Guidelines

Acceptable Items

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Cooked meals (within 2 hours)
  • Packaged food (unopened)
  • Grains, rice, and pulses
  • Dairy products (properly stored)
  • Bakery items (fresh)

Not Acceptable

  • Expired or spoiled food
  • Food left out for more than 2 hours
  • Opened packaged items
  • Alcohol or tobacco products
  • Non-food items
  • Food with unknown ingredients
