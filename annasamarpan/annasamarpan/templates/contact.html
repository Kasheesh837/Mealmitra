Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I donate food?

Simply fill out our donation form with details about the food you want to donate. Include the type, quantity, and pickup location. We'll assign a volunteer to collect it.

How can I become a volunteer?

Register as a volunteer by filling out our volunteer form. You'll receive email notifications when donations are available in your area for pickup and delivery.

What types of food can I donate?

We accept fresh fruits, vegetables, cooked meals (within 2 hours), packaged food (unopened), grains, rice, dairy products, and bakery items. All food must be safe for consumption.

How do I know if I'm eligible to receive food?

Recipients are verified by our admin team. If you're in need of food assistance, please contact us directly, and we'll help you through the verification process.

Is there any cost involved?

No, our platform is completely free for donors, volunteers, and recipients. We're committed to making food sharing accessible to everyone.
