Our Mission

"No meal should go wasted, no hand should go empty" - This is the driving force behind everything we do.

Our Core Mission

To eliminate food waste and hunger by creating a seamless connection between food donors, volunteers, and recipients. We believe that technology can amplify human compassion to solve one of society's most pressing challenges.

Prevent Food Waste

We connect surplus food with those who need it most, ensuring that good food doesn't end up in landfills.

Fight Hunger

We ensure that nutritious food reaches families and individuals who are struggling with food insecurity.

Build Community

We foster connections between neighbors, creating stronger, more caring communities.

The Problem We're Solving

Massive Food Waste

Approximately 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted globally each year, while millions go hungry.

Food Insecurity

Over 800 million people worldwide suffer from hunger, including many in our own communities.

Lack of Connection

There's often no easy way for donors to connect with those in need, leading to missed opportunities.

The Numbers

Food wasted annually: 1.3B tons
People going hungry: 800M+
Environmental impact: 8% GHG
Economic loss: $1T+
