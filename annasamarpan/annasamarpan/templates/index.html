How It Works

Our simple three-step process makes it easy to donate food and help those in need

1. Donate Food

Submit details about your food donation including type, quantity, and pickup location. We'll handle the rest.

2. Volunteer Assignment

Our system automatically assigns nearby volunteers to pick up and deliver your donation to verified recipients.

3. Impact Made

Track the impact of your donation and see how many meals you've helped provide to families in need.
